About Me

Hey there! I’m Chris — born and raised in Colorado, recently back in the US after a few incredible years living in Sydney, Australia.

My love for photography started with a love for travel, the outdoors, and chasing new experiences. I gave professional photography a shot full-time for a bit, but quickly realized it took the joy out of it. Being boxed into certain types of work and clients made it feel more like a chore than a creative outlet.

So, I took a step back. These days, photography is still a huge part of my life — but now, I get to do it on my own terms. I take on the projects that excite me, work with people I genuinely enjoy, and charge what feels fair without the pressure of needing to make rent from every gig.

For me, photography is about connection — with people, places, and moments. If you’ve got a project in mind and need someone to bring a bit of creative energy to the table, let’s chat. I’d love to help bring your vision to life.
